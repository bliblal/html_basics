Paragraphs

this is how to create paragraphs.

use p tag

Headings

Use h tags. h1 h2 h3... h6 are different levels of headings.

Bold

strong tag is used to bold text. it also creates a tone of emphasis for visual impairment tools

em (emphasis) can also be used.

Nesting Tags

nesting is possible. outer tag is parent, inner is child
